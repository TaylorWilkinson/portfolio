about

Hello there! I am a normal human woman (definitely not a nursery of raccoons in a human suit) that codes for a living. My career in web development began in 2013 when I was playing with custom CSS on my secret One Direction tumblr blog. Once I realized “Wow, people do this for a living!” (“this” being coding and web design, not reposting 2010s celeb photos), it propelled me to apply to SFU’s SIAT program - an interdisciplinary school that covered development, design, and media. I graduated in 2019 with my BSc. in Interactive Systems.

Fast forward to now, I am working as a web developer in-house on a marketing team, working with internal stakeholders and marketers to maintain their website and manage web content. I still love playing with CSS to this day. Other tools and stacks I’ve used include Javascript, HTML5, React, Gatsby.js, Vue.js, and GraphQL.
