brief

A month long project to design a website for a fictional company of our making, with an emphasis on responsive design. (IAT 339). 2018.

Project requirements included:

  • Company styleguide, including grid structure and colour swatches
  • Content using real language (i.e. no Lorem Ipsum) appropriate for the company, it's voice, and the products it sells, including: company updates/events, about the company and multi-product/multi-service listings
  • Determine and build a sitemap to illustrate page hierarchy and the links between pages
  • Working with git, and proof of team members work on their individual branches
